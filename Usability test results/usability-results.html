Usability Test Results

Three significant observations

  • The typewriting effect attracted users' attention and set the tone effectively.
  • Users were confused about the second scene and how the choices could influence the story.
  • Users got distracted in the third scene and did not know how it related to the final results.

How the project can be improved

First scene:

  • Add more background animation effects to enhance the atmosphere, such as glitch lines or color distortion.

Second scene:

  • Add one more choice to ensure all elements are evenly aligned and visually balanced.
  • Add a yellow highlight and a left-side triangle icon as a hover effect for each choice to enhance visual feedback and interactivity.
  • When the user clicks the “Repair” button, the card will change to green. If the user selects “Discard,” the card will turn red, visually indicating the outcome of their choice.
  • Add a scientist's voice recording to the scientist audio card to provide background context and deepen the narrative of the project.
  • Enlarge the letter size in the letter from future human card.
  • Change another way to interact with the AI-human love memory card.
  • Rethink the narrative structure of the system report log to make it more coherent and meaningful.

Third scene:

  • Add subtle sound effects to enhance the atmosphere and emphasize the weight of each choice
  • redesign the UI of the choice buttons.

Ending scene:

  • Make the data visualized: how user's choice will affect the ending.
  • Write a concluding scene to complete the narrative to make the story more cohesive and complete.
